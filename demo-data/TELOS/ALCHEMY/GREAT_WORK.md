# The Great Work

> The opus magnum — the long arc of the work across time.

---

## Three Stages

Following the alchemical model: Nigredo (dissolution and confronting what is), Albedo (purification and clarity), Rubedo (synthesis and embodied action).

---

## Nigredo — Current Stage

**Theme:** Confronting the gap between potential and execution.

I have a clear sense of what good work looks like and significant gaps in consistently delivering it. Client work is solid; personal projects accumulate debt. The challenge is not vision — it is discipline, system, and follow-through.

The Nigredo work is:
- Building sustainable systems (PAI Data UI is an example — tooling that supports the work rather than adding to the chaos)
- Reducing the gap between what I say I will do and what I do
- Confronting the avoidance patterns (scope creep, over-promising, under-resting)

---

## Albedo — The Vision

**Theme:** Clarity of craft and contribution.

A practice of work where the quality of the output reliably reflects the quality of the intention. Where the systems I use support rather than complicate. Where I am known for a specific, deep form of work rather than a broad, shallow range.

The Albedo work will be:
- A body of tools and systems that I use, maintain, and share openly
- A small network of long-term client relationships built on demonstrated value
- A personal practice (reading, meditation, physical) that sustains the work sustainably

---

## Rubedo — The Long Horizon

**Theme:** Transmission and contribution.

The work leaves something behind that outlasts the individual engagement. Open source tools with real adoption. Documentation that teaches. A way of working that others can learn from and adapt.

Not fame — usefulness. Not scale — depth.

---

## What This Means Today

The Great Work is not a future project. It is the accumulated quality of today's decisions:

- Does this deliverable have integrity?
- Does this system serve the person or create dependency?
- Is this documentation good enough for a stranger to maintain?
- Did I protect my focused time today?
