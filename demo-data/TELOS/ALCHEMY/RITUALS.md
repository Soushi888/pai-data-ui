# Rituals

> Daily and seasonal practices that structure and sustain the work.

---

## Daily

**Morning (before screen)**
- 15 minutes meditation — breath-focused, no guided audio
- Physical movement: 10 minutes (stretching or a short walk)
- Review today's focus list before opening email or messages

**Work blocks**
- Deep work: 9:00–12:00 (protected, no notifications, no meetings when possible)
- Admin and communication: 14:00–16:00
- No context-switching within a deep work block — a problem that would require more than 20 minutes gets a task card, not immediate action

**Evening**
- 10-15 minutes of reading (non-screen, physical book)
- Brief note of what was completed and what carries to tomorrow
- Guitar or another non-productive activity before sleep

---

## Weekly

- Sunday evening: review focus list, set priorities for the week, brief TELOS STATUS check
- Friday afternoon: review completed tasks, invoice any work not yet billed, check outstanding receivables

---

## Monthly

- First Monday: review GOALS.md and CHALLENGES.md — are the priorities still right?
- Mid-month: financial review — income, expenses, outstanding invoices
- End of month: update UPDATES.md with any significant TELOS changes

---

## Seasonal / Quarterly

- Full TELOS review: re-read all core files, update STATUS.md comprehensively
- Project retrospectives: what worked, what did not, what to carry forward
- Rate and positioning review: is my positioning still accurate? Does my rate still reflect my value?
