# Beliefs

> Core worldview principles. These are working hypotheses, not dogmas.

---

## Craft Is a Form of Ethics

How something is made matters as much as what it does. Sloppy code, careless design, and rushed deliverables are not just quality problems — they are ethical failures. Someone else will maintain this. Someone else will use this. That person deserves your best effort.

## Autonomy Over Convenience

Given a choice between a tool that is convenient but opaque and one that is slightly harder but fully owned, choose ownership. Convenience is a subscription; autonomy compounds.

## Complexity Hides Poor Thinking

When a system is hard to explain, it is usually because the thinking behind it is incomplete. The goal is not simplicity for its own sake — it is clarity. Simple is what you get when the thinking is done.

## Small Teams Build Better Things

The best work I have seen comes from small, high-trust teams with clear mandates. Large committees produce compromises; small groups produce decisions. This applies to code, design, and governance.

## Open Source Is Infrastructure, Not Ideology

Open source software is critical infrastructure. Treating it as a political statement misses the point. It is an engineering approach with specific tradeoffs — and for foundational tools, those tradeoffs usually favor openness.

## The Client Is Not Always Right, But They Are Always the Decision-Maker

My job is to provide expert counsel, push back when I see a mistake, and then implement what is decided. The decision belongs to the client. This is not a contradiction — it is respect for the relationship.

## Local-First Is the Right Default for Personal Tools

Data that lives on your machine, in open formats, that you can read with a text editor — this is not nostalgia. It is the most resilient architecture for personal and small-team tooling.

## Rest Is Productive

Sustained focus requires recovery. The culture that celebrates overwork is not high-performance — it is high-burnout. I protect sleep, breaks, and disconnection as seriously as I protect deep work time.
