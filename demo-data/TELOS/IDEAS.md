# Ideas

> Uncommitted project ideas. Not plans — seeds worth keeping visible.

---

## Design System Registry (Open Source)

A shareable, version-controlled design token and component library format that works across frameworks. Not another UI kit — a specification format that any tool can consume. The problem: design tokens are everywhere; interoperability is nowhere.

**Status:** Thinking stage. Observing if someone else builds this first.

---

## Invoice-to-CRM Automation Layer

A lightweight tool that reads paid invoice history and automatically updates CRM opportunity status, contact last-touch dates, and project records. Reduces the manual sync between ERP and CRM data. Could be built as a PAI Data UI plugin.

**Status:** Half-built in my head. Would solve my own workflow immediately.

---

## "Design Audit" as a Productized Service

A fixed-scope, fixed-price design audit for small product teams: 5 days, 10 user interface pages, documented findings and recommendations. Predictable for clients, efficient for me. No custom scoping, no negotiation.

**Status:** Interested. Waiting until Lumina Phase 3 is complete before adding new service lines.

---

## Personal Knowledge Graph (Local)

A local-first tool for visualizing connections between TELOS files, project notes, and research — like Obsidian's graph but simpler and integrated with PAI data. Not a replacement for search; a supplement for serendipitous discovery.

**Status:** Nascent. SQLite FTS + a D3 force graph might be enough.

---

## Freelance Rate Transparency Resource

A published dataset (anonymized, opt-in) for freelance design and development rates in Montreal, by domain and experience level. The market has no price transparency; junior practitioners are consistently underpaid because they have no reference point.

**Status:** Interested but uncertain about the effort-to-impact ratio.
